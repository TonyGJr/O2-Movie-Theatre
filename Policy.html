O2 Multiplex Theatre Policy

Age Policy for R-Rated Films

  • Patrons under 17 years of age must be accompanied by their parents or an adult guardian (21 years or older)
  • Patrons 25 years or younger must show ID
  • We, the O2 Multiplex Theatre Family, restrict children younger than 6 years of age to attend R-Rated films after 6pm to improve the experience for everone

No outside Food and Berverages Allowed

  • Please refrain from bringing outside food and berverage items into the theatre

Refund Policy

  • No refuns after printed showtime

Alcohol Carding Policy

  • Regardless of age, all guests purchasing or consuming alcohol within the theatre must show positive proof of age
